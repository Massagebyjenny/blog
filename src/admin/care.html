Patient care

What to Expect

Care is provided in a quiet, private clinical setting designed to support focus and nervous system ease.

Sessions are individualized and responsive, guided by ongoing assessment rather than predetermined routines.

Who this is for

Therapeutic, individualized work

This work is often sought by individuals experiencing persistent strain or imbalance and looking for a more therapeutic approach than routine massage.

Many patients pursue this work after other approaches have provided limited or temporary relief.

Intensity

Effective, appropriate intensity

The work is intentional and precise, and may involve focused therapeutic intensity appropriate to the tissue.

Open communication is encouraged throughout each session to keep the work both effective and appropriate.

Clinical evaluation

How care begins

New patients begin with a Neuro-Structural Evaluation. This establishes the foundation for individualized treatment and helps determine a productive direction for care.
