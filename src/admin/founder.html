Founder

Jenny Wiersma, LMT

Licensed since 2004

Jenny Wiersma has devoted her career to hands-on therapeutic work grounded in clinical attentiveness and structural understanding.

Her work is defined by precision, thoughtful pacing, and the ability to identify patterns of restriction that often go unaddressed in routine massage models.

The development of Neuro-Structural Alignment™

A method revealed through practice

The method emerged through years of observation as consistent structural patterns appeared across diverse bodies.

Over time, these patterns formed a coherent approach centered on how tension, compensation, and neuromuscular response influence overall function.

Rather than being constructed as a theory-first system, Neuro-Structural Alignment™ developed through active clinical work and real patient response.

Ongoing practice

Grounded in active clinical work

Jenny continues to practice full-time in Delray Beach, refining the clinical approach that defines Neuro-Structural Alignment™.

Any future educational or professional offerings will remain rooted in this active clinical foundation.
