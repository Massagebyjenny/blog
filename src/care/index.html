Care

What to expect

Sessions are evaluation‑led and individualized. The goal is to reduce the tension pattern that is driving load and guarding, then help your body keep that change.

Step 1

Intake & goals

We start with your main concerns, history, and what you want to be able to do comfortably. If you have imaging or clinician notes, those can be helpful context.

Step 2

Structural assessment

Posture, breathing mechanics, and key movement patterns may be observed to identify where the body is compensating. This helps target the work efficiently.

Step 3

Hands‑on treatment

Treatment blends gentle fascial release with specific neuromuscular work. Pressure is not the point — response is. The session is paced to reduce guarding rather than provoke it.

After the session

Integration & follow‑through

You may notice changes immediately (ease of movement, breathing, balance) and/or gradually over days. When helpful, you’ll receive simple guidance — often about movement, hydration, and rest — to support the new baseline.

Because every body is different, the number of sessions needed varies. Care is planned based on response, not packages.
