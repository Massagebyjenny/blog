Neuro-Structural Alignment™

Structural realignment therapy for persistent pain, posture, and movement restrictions

This work blends gentle fascial release, structural reasoning, and nervous system down‑shifting to unwind whole‑body tension patterns — so your body can move with less guarding and load. It is precise, individualized care, not routine “deep tissue.”

Request an evaluation Explore the method What to expect

Note: This site describes a therapeutic bodywork approach. It does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, or guarantees.

Core idea

Work the pattern, not just the symptom

Pain is often where the body is overloaded — not always where the restriction begins. Sessions look for the tension and compensation pattern driving the load, then unwind it through targeted fascial and neuromuscular work.

What clients notice

More ease, better balance

Many clients report improved posture, freer breathing, more comfortable walking/sitting, and reduced “pull” through areas that felt chronically tight — often without aggressive pressure.

Results vary by person and condition.
