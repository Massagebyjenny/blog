Professionals

For healthcare & bodywork professionals

Neuro‑Structural Alignment™ supports clients with persistent musculoskeletal tension patterns where posture, mechanics, and nervous system guarding appear to be contributing factors.

Referrals

Collaborative care

Professional referrals are welcome. When appropriate, care can be coordinated with physical therapy, chiropractic, medical providers, and other bodywork disciplines.

Communication

Clear feedback loops

Sessions can be guided by functional goals and practical outcomes (e.g., gait comfort, sitting tolerance, range of motion), with progress communicated in plain language.

Future education

Method development

The long‑term direction includes careful development of educational pathways rooted in active clinical practice. Inquiries are welcomed as availability permits.
