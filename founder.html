Founder

Jenny Wiersma, LMT

Neuro‑Structural Alignment™ was shaped through years of hands‑on clinical practice and a consistent question: “What is the pattern underneath the pain — and how do we change it without the body fighting back?”

Clinical focus

Complex, recurring patterns

Many clients arrive after trying standard massage, stretching, or isolated treatments that provided only temporary relief. This work focuses on the structural pattern and nervous system tone that keep symptoms returning.

Style

Precise, not forceful

The method prioritizes specificity, pacing, and tissue response. It is designed to create change without “winning a fight” against the body.

Philosophy

Alignment is functional

“Alignment” here is not about perfect posture — it’s about restoring efficient mechanics so you can move, work, and live with less strain.

Care first

Thoughtful pacing

Because care is individualized and thoughtfully paced, availability is limited and response times may vary. If your inquiry is time‑sensitive, calling is best.
